# SignCraft vs Adobe Sign (Acrobat Sign)

Looking for a **free, open-source alternative to Adobe Sign**? Here's how SignCraft compares.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | SignCraft | Adobe Sign |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| Electronic Signatures | Yes | Yes |
| AI Contract Review | Yes — clause-by-clause risk analysis | No |
| AI Contract Drafting | Yes — generate contracts from a prompt | No |
| Legal Templates | 9 built-in (NDA, SAFE, SaaS, etc.) | Template library (paid) |
| Self-Hosted | Yes — your data stays on your server | No — Adobe cloud only |
| Open Source | Yes — MIT license | No — proprietary |
| Embeddable Widget | Yes — embed signing in any website | Limited |
| Pricing | Free | $15-30/month per user |

## Why Choose SignCraft Over Adobe Sign?

### 1. AI Built In — Not an Upsell
Adobe is adding AI features to Acrobat, but contract review and drafting aren't part of the signing workflow. SignCraft integrates AI directly into the drafting and review process.

### 2. No Adobe Ecosystem Lock-in
Adobe Sign works best when you're already paying for Creative Cloud or Acrobat Pro. SignCraft is standalone — no ecosystem dependencies.

### 3. Self-Hosted
Adobe Sign is cloud-only. SignCraft runs on your own infrastructure, keeping sensitive contracts off third-party servers.

### 4. Free & Open Source
Adobe Sign costs $15-30/user/month. SignCraft is free, MIT-licensed, and self-hostable.

### 5. Developer-Friendly
Built with Next.js, Supabase, and TipTap. Easy to customize, extend, and integrate with your existing stack.

## When Adobe Sign Might Be Better

- You're already deep in the Adobe ecosystem
- You need certified digital signatures (PKI-based)
- You require enterprise compliance and audit certifications
- You need Adobe's document management features (Forms, PDF editing)
