# SignCraft vs DocuSign

Looking for a **free, open-source alternative to DocuSign**? Here's how SignCraft compares.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | SignCraft | DocuSign |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Electronic Signatures | Yes | Yes |
| AI Contract Review | Yes — clause-by-clause risk analysis | No |
| AI Contract Drafting | Yes — generate contracts from a prompt | No |
| Legal Templates | 9 built-in (NDA, SAFE, SaaS, etc.) | Limited free templates |
| Self-Hosted | Yes — your data stays on your server | No — cloud only |
| Open Source | Yes — MIT license | No — proprietary |
| Embeddable Widget | Yes — embed signing in any website | API only (expensive) |
| Audit Trail | Yes — IP, timestamp, user agent | Yes |
| Pricing | Free | $10-60/month per user |

## Why Choose SignCraft Over DocuSign?

### 1. AI-Powered Contract Intelligence
DocuSign collects signatures. SignCraft **reviews your contracts before you sign**. Every clause gets analyzed for risk — green (standard), yellow (needs attention), red (potentially unfavorable). DocuSign doesn't offer this at any price tier.

### 2. No Per-User Pricing
DocuSign charges $10-60 per user per month. SignCraft is free and self-hosted — send unlimited documents to unlimited signers.

### 3. Self-Hosted & Private
Your contracts contain sensitive business information. With SignCraft, that data never leaves your infrastructure. DocuSign stores everything on their servers.

### 4. Open Source (MIT License)
Fork it, modify it, white-label it, embed it in your product. MIT license means no restrictions — unlike AGPL alternatives that limit commercial use.

### 5. AI Drafting
Need an NDA in 30 seconds? Describe what you need and SignCraft generates it. DocuSign requires you to upload pre-written documents.

## When DocuSign Might Be Better

- You need enterprise compliance certifications (SOC 2 Type II, FedRAMP)
- You require advanced workflow routing with dozens of signers
- You need certified notarization services
- Your organization mandates a vendor-supported SLA
