# SignCraft vs PandaDoc

Looking for a **free, open-source alternative to PandaDoc**? Here's how SignCraft compares.

## Feature Comparison

| Feature | SignCraft | PandaDoc |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| Electronic Signatures | Yes | Yes |
| AI Contract Review | Yes — clause-by-clause risk analysis | No |
| AI Contract Drafting | Yes — generate contracts from a prompt | AI assist (paid tiers) |
| Legal Templates | 9 built-in (NDA, SAFE, SaaS, etc.) | Template library (paid) |
| Self-Hosted | Yes — your data stays on your server | No — cloud only |
| Open Source | Yes — MIT license | No — proprietary |
| CRM Integration | Not yet | HubSpot, Salesforce, etc. |
| Pricing | Free | $19-65/month per user |

## Why Choose SignCraft Over PandaDoc?

### 1. AI Contract Review — Not Just Drafting
PandaDoc has added AI drafting to higher tiers, but it doesn't review contracts for risk. SignCraft analyzes every clause with green/yellow/red risk flags before you sign.

### 2. No Per-Seat Pricing
PandaDoc's business plan costs $49/user/month. For a 10-person team, that's $490/month. SignCraft is free for unlimited users.

### 3. Self-Hosted & Private
PandaDoc stores all documents on their servers. SignCraft runs on your infrastructure — your contracts, your data, your control.

### 4. MIT License
PandaDoc is proprietary. SignCraft is open source under MIT — fork it, modify it, embed it in your product. No restrictions.

### 5. Legal-First Templates
SignCraft's templates are designed for legal contracts (NDA, SAFE, IP Assignment), not sales proposals. Different tools for different jobs.

## When PandaDoc Might Be Better

- You need CRM integrations (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- You create sales proposals and quotes (not just contracts)
- You need payment collection built into documents
- You want a managed service with support SLAs
