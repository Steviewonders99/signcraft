# ADVISOR AGREEMENT

**Effective Date:** {{Effective Date}}

This Advisor Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

**{{Company Name}}** ("Company"), and

**{{Advisor Name}}** ("Advisor").

Company and Advisor are each referred to herein as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

## 1. Background

Company desires to engage Advisor to provide strategic advice and guidance, and Advisor desires to provide such advisory services, subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

## 2. Advisory Services

2.1. **Scope.** Advisor shall provide the following advisory services to Company (the "Services"): (a) strategic guidance on business development, product direction, and market positioning; (b) introductions to potential customers, partners, and investors, as appropriate; (c) participation in periodic advisory meetings or calls as reasonably requested by Company; and (d) such other advisory services as the Parties may mutually agree upon from time to time.

2.2. **Time Commitment.** Advisor shall devote approximately five (5) hours per month to the Services. The Parties acknowledge that Advisor's time commitment may vary based on Company's needs and Advisor's availability.

2.3. **Independent Contractor.** Advisor is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of Company. Advisor shall have no authority to bind Company or incur obligations on Company's behalf. Advisor shall not be entitled to any employee benefits from Company.

## 3. Compensation

3.1. **Equity Grant.** As compensation for the Services, Company shall grant Advisor an equity interest equal to {{Equity Percentage}} percent ({{Equity Percentage}}%) of Company's fully diluted capitalization as of the Effective Date (the "Equity Grant"), subject to the vesting schedule set forth in Section 3.2.

3.2. **Vesting.** The Equity Grant shall vest over a period of {{Vesting Period Months}} months from the Effective Date (the "Vesting Period"), with no cliff, vesting in equal monthly installments at the end of each calendar month of continuous service. Vesting shall cease immediately upon termination of this Agreement for any reason.

3.3. **Equity Documentation.** The Equity Grant shall be subject to the terms and conditions of Company's standard equity incentive plan and a separate equity agreement, which shall be delivered to Advisor within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date. In the event of a conflict between this Agreement and the equity agreement, the equity agreement shall control with respect to the terms of the Equity Grant.

3.4. **No Cash Compensation.** Unless otherwise agreed in writing, Advisor shall not receive any cash compensation for the Services. Company shall not reimburse Advisor for expenses unless pre-approved in writing by Company.

## 4. Term

4.1. **Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period equal to the Vesting Period, unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 8 (the "Term").

4.2. **Renewal.** Upon expiration of the Term, this Agreement may be renewed by mutual written agreement of the Parties.

## 5. Confidentiality

5.1. **Definition.** "Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by Company to Advisor, whether orally, in writing, or electronically, that is designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure. Confidential Information includes, without limitation, business plans, financial data, product roadmaps, customer information, technology, trade secrets, and fundraising activities.

5.2. **Obligations.** Advisor shall: (a) use Confidential Information solely for the purpose of providing the Services; (b) protect Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care Advisor uses for Advisor's own confidential information, but no less than reasonable care; (c) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without Company's prior written consent; and (d) return or destroy all Confidential Information upon termination of this Agreement.

5.3. **Exclusions.** Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of Advisor; (b) was known to Advisor before disclosure by Company; (c) is independently developed by Advisor without use of Company's Confidential Information; or (d) is rightfully received from a third party without restriction.

5.4. **Duration.** The obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination of this Agreement and continue for a period of three (3) years from the date of disclosure, or indefinitely for trade secrets as long as they remain trade secrets under applicable law.

## 6. Intellectual Property Assignment

6.1. **Assignment.** Advisor hereby irrevocably assigns to Company all right, title, and interest in and to any inventions, ideas, improvements, works of authorship, and other intellectual property that Advisor creates, conceives, or develops in connection with the Services ("Advisor Work Product"), including all intellectual property rights therein.

6.2. **Pre-Existing IP.** Advisor retains ownership of any intellectual property that Advisor owned prior to this Agreement or develops independently outside the scope of this Agreement. To the extent any such pre-existing intellectual property is incorporated into Advisor Work Product, Advisor grants Company a non-exclusive, perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free license to use such intellectual property solely as part of the Advisor Work Product.

6.3. **Further Assurances.** Advisor shall execute any documents and take any actions reasonably necessary to perfect Company's ownership of the Advisor Work Product.

## 7. Non-Compete and Non-Solicitation

7.1. **Non-Solicitation.** During the Term and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination (the "Restricted Period"), Advisor shall not, directly or indirectly, solicit, recruit, or hire any employee or contractor of Company, or induce any such person to terminate their relationship with Company.

7.2. **Conflicts of Interest.** Advisor shall promptly disclose to Company any existing or potential conflicts of interest, including advisory or employment relationships with companies that compete with Company. Company acknowledges that Advisor may provide advisory services to other companies and that this Agreement does not restrict Advisor from doing so, provided Advisor complies with the confidentiality obligations in Section 5.

## 8. Termination

8.1. **Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement for any reason upon fifteen (15) days' prior written notice to the other Party.

8.2. **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within ten (10) days after receiving written notice thereof.

8.3. **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (a) Advisor shall cease providing the Services; (b) vesting of the Equity Grant shall cease as of the effective date of termination; (c) Advisor shall return or destroy all of Company's Confidential Information; and (d) Sections 5, 6, 7, and 9 shall survive termination.

## 9. General Provisions

9.1. **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of {{Governing Law State}}, without regard to its conflict of laws provisions.

9.2. **Dispute Resolution.** Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively in the state or federal courts located in the State of {{Governing Law State}}, and each Party irrevocably consents to the jurisdiction of such courts.

9.3. **Notices.** All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be deemed given when: (a) delivered personally; (b) sent by confirmed email; or (c) sent by nationally recognized overnight courier.

9.4. **Assignment.** Advisor may not assign this Agreement. Company may assign this Agreement to an affiliate or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of its assets.

9.5. **Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect. The Parties shall negotiate in good faith to replace any invalid provision with a valid provision that achieves the original intent to the greatest extent permitted by law.

9.6. **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, negotiations, and discussions, whether oral or written.

9.7. **Amendment.** No amendment or modification of this Agreement shall be effective unless made in writing and signed by both Parties.

9.8. **Waiver.** The failure of either Party to enforce any provision of this Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of such Party's right to enforce that provision or any other provision in the future.

9.9. **Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one agreement.
