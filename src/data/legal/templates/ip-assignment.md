# INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

**Effective Date:** {{Effective Date}}

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

**{{Assignor Name}}** ("Assignor"), and

**{{Assignee Name}}** ("Assignee").

Assignor and Assignee are each referred to herein as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

## 1. Background

Assignor owns certain intellectual property that Assignee desires to acquire, and Assignor desires to assign such intellectual property to Assignee, subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

## 2. Assignment of Rights

2.1. **Assigned IP.** The intellectual property subject to this Agreement (the "Assigned IP") is described as follows: {{Description of IP}}.

2.2. **Assignment.** Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Assignee all of Assignor's right, title, and interest in and to the Assigned IP, including without limitation:

(a) all patents, patent applications, and patent rights, including all continuations, continuations-in-part, divisionals, reissues, and re-examinations;

(b) all copyrights, copyright registrations, and copyright applications, including all rights of reproduction, distribution, display, performance, and the creation of derivative works;

(c) all trademarks, trademark registrations, trademark applications, trade names, service marks, and associated goodwill;

(d) all trade secrets, know-how, inventions, processes, techniques, algorithms, software code (including source code and object code), data, databases, designs, drawings, specifications, and documentation;

(e) all other intellectual property and proprietary rights of any kind or nature, whether registered or unregistered, and whether arising under the laws of any jurisdiction; and

(f) all rights to sue and recover for past, present, and future infringement, misappropriation, or other violation of any of the foregoing.

2.3. **Effective Transfer.** The assignment set forth in Section 2.2 shall be effective as of the Effective Date and shall be perpetual and irrevocable.

## 3. Consideration

3.1. **Payment.** In consideration of the assignment of the Assigned IP, Assignee shall pay Assignor a one-time payment of {{Consideration Amount}} (the "Consideration"), payable within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date.

3.2. **Sufficiency.** Each Party acknowledges that the Consideration is fair, adequate, and sufficient.

## 4. Representations and Warranties

4.1. **Assignor Representations.** Assignor represents and warrants that:

(a) Assignor is the sole and exclusive owner of the Assigned IP and has full right, power, and authority to assign the Assigned IP to Assignee;

(b) the Assigned IP is free and clear of all liens, encumbrances, security interests, licenses, and claims of any third party;

(c) to Assignor's knowledge, the Assigned IP does not infringe, misappropriate, or otherwise violate the intellectual property rights of any third party;

(d) there are no pending or threatened claims, actions, suits, or proceedings relating to the Assigned IP;

(e) Assignor has not previously assigned, transferred, licensed, or encumbered any of the rights being assigned under this Agreement;

(f) Assignor has the full power and authority to enter into and perform this Agreement, and the execution of this Agreement does not conflict with any other agreement to which Assignor is a party; and

(g) all persons who contributed to the creation of the Assigned IP have executed valid assignments of their rights to Assignor, or such contributions constitute works made for hire owned by Assignor.

4.2. **Assignee Representations.** Assignee represents and warrants that:

(a) Assignee has full power and authority to enter into and perform this Agreement; and

(b) the execution and performance of this Agreement does not conflict with any other agreement to which Assignee is a party.

## 5. Further Assurances

5.1. **Cooperation.** Assignor shall, at Assignee's request and expense, execute and deliver any additional documents, instruments, or agreements, and take any further actions reasonably necessary to: (a) perfect Assignee's ownership of the Assigned IP; (b) register or record the assignment with any governmental authority or intellectual property office; (c) apply for, obtain, or maintain any patent, copyright registration, or trademark registration relating to the Assigned IP; and (d) enforce or defend Assignee's rights in the Assigned IP.

5.2. **Power of Attorney.** If Assignor fails or is unable to execute any document necessary to perfect Assignee's ownership of the Assigned IP, Assignor hereby irrevocably appoints Assignee as Assignor's attorney-in-fact, with full power and authority to execute such documents on Assignor's behalf. This power of attorney is coupled with an interest and is irrevocable.

5.3. **Records and Materials.** Within fifteen (15) days of the Effective Date, Assignor shall deliver to Assignee all tangible embodiments of the Assigned IP in Assignor's possession or control, including all source code, documentation, designs, prototypes, research materials, and related records.

## 6. Indemnification

6.1. **Assignor Indemnification.** Assignor shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Assignee and its officers, directors, employees, and agents from and against any third-party claims, damages, losses, liabilities, costs, and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or relating to: (a) any breach of Assignor's representations or warranties under this Agreement; or (b) any claim that the Assigned IP infringes or misappropriates a third party's intellectual property rights, to the extent such infringement or misappropriation existed prior to the Effective Date.

6.2. **Indemnification Procedure.** The indemnified Party shall: (a) promptly notify the indemnifying Party in writing of any claim; (b) grant the indemnifying Party sole control of the defense and settlement; and (c) provide reasonable cooperation at the indemnifying Party's expense.

## 7. General Provisions

7.1. **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of {{Governing Law State}}, without regard to its conflict of laws provisions.

7.2. **Dispute Resolution.** Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively in the state or federal courts located in the State of {{Governing Law State}}, and each Party irrevocably consents to the jurisdiction of such courts.

7.3. **Notices.** All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be deemed given when: (a) delivered personally; (b) sent by confirmed email; or (c) sent by nationally recognized overnight courier.

7.4. **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other Party, except that Assignee may assign this Agreement without consent to an affiliate or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of its assets.

7.5. **Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

7.6. **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, negotiations, and discussions, whether oral or written.

7.7. **Amendment.** No amendment or modification of this Agreement shall be effective unless made in writing and signed by both Parties.

7.8. **Waiver.** The failure of either Party to enforce any provision of this Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of such Party's right to enforce that provision or any other provision in the future.

7.9. **Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original, and all of which together shall constitute one agreement.
