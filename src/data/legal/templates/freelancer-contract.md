# FREELANCER CONTRACT

This Freelancer Contract ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

**{{Client Name}}** ("Client"), and

**{{Freelancer Name}}** ("Freelancer").

Client and Freelancer are each referred to herein as a "Party" and collectively as the "Parties."

## 1. Project Scope

1.1. **Project Description.** Client engages Freelancer to perform the following project (the "Project"): {{Project Description}}.

1.2. **Standard of Performance.** Freelancer shall perform the Project in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with generally accepted industry standards.

## 2. Deliverables

2.1. **Deliverables.** Freelancer shall deliver the following items to Client (the "Deliverables"): {{Deliverables}}.

2.2. **Acceptance.** Client shall review each Deliverable within ten (10) business days of receipt ("Review Period"). If Client does not provide written notice of rejection with specific reasons during the Review Period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted. If Client rejects a Deliverable, Freelancer shall revise and resubmit the Deliverable within ten (10) business days.

## 3. Compensation

3.1. **Fee.** Client shall pay Freelancer a total fee of {{Fee Amount}} for the Project (the "Fee").

3.2. **Payment Schedule.** {{Payment Schedule}}.

3.3. **Late Payments.** Late payments shall accrue interest at the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law.

3.4. **Taxes.** Freelancer shall be solely responsible for all taxes arising from compensation paid under this Agreement.

## 4. Timeline

4.1. **Deadline.** Freelancer shall complete and deliver all Deliverables no later than {{Deadline}} (the "Deadline").

4.2. **Delays.** If Freelancer anticipates that the Deadline will not be met, Freelancer shall promptly notify Client in writing and propose a revised timeline. Client may, at its sole discretion, accept the revised timeline or terminate this Agreement pursuant to Section 8.

4.3. **Client Cooperation.** Client shall provide all information, materials, and approvals reasonably necessary for Freelancer to perform the Project. Delays caused by Client's failure to provide required materials shall extend the Deadline by an equal number of days.

## 5. Revisions

5.1. **Included Revisions.** The Fee includes up to two (2) rounds of revisions for each Deliverable. A "revision" means modifications to an accepted or submitted Deliverable based on Client's written feedback.

5.2. **Additional Revisions.** Additional revisions beyond the included rounds shall be billed at Freelancer's standard hourly rate, to be agreed upon in writing before work begins.

## 6. Intellectual Property Assignment

6.1. **Assignment.** Upon receipt of full payment, Freelancer hereby irrevocably assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in and to the Deliverables and all work product created in connection with the Project ("Work Product"), including all intellectual property rights therein.

6.2. **Pre-Existing IP.** Freelancer retains ownership of any intellectual property that Freelancer owned prior to this Agreement or develops independently outside the scope of this Agreement ("Freelancer IP"). To the extent any Freelancer IP is incorporated into the Work Product, Freelancer grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, modify, and distribute such Freelancer IP solely as part of the Work Product.

6.3. **Portfolio Rights.** Freelancer may display the Deliverables in Freelancer's portfolio for self-promotional purposes, unless Client provides written notice prohibiting such use.

6.4. **Further Assurances.** Freelancer shall execute any documents and take any actions reasonably necessary to perfect Client's ownership of the Work Product.

## 7. Confidentiality

7.1. **Obligations.** Each Party shall keep confidential all non-public information received from the other Party in connection with this Agreement ("Confidential Information"). Neither Party shall use or disclose Confidential Information except as necessary to perform its obligations under this Agreement.

7.2. **Exclusions.** Confidential Information does not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was known to the Receiving Party before disclosure; (c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party; or (d) is rightfully received from a third party without restriction.

7.3. **Duration.** The obligations of confidentiality shall survive termination of this Agreement and continue for a period of two (2) years from the date of disclosure.

## 8. Termination

8.1. **Termination by Client.** Client may terminate this Agreement at any time upon written notice to Freelancer. Upon termination by Client: (a) Client shall pay Freelancer for all work completed and accepted through the date of termination; and (b) Freelancer shall deliver all completed and in-progress Work Product to Client.

8.2. **Termination by Freelancer.** Freelancer may terminate this Agreement upon fifteen (15) days' written notice if Client fails to make any payment when due and does not cure such failure within ten (10) days after receiving written notice thereof.

8.3. **Survival.** Sections 6, 7, 9, 10, and 11 shall survive termination of this Agreement.

## 9. Independent Contractor

9.1. **Status.** Freelancer is an independent contractor and not an employee, agent, partner, or joint venturer of Client. Freelancer shall have no authority to bind Client or incur obligations on Client's behalf.

9.2. **No Benefits.** Freelancer shall not be entitled to any employee benefits from Client.

9.3. **Control.** Freelancer shall determine the method, details, and means of performing the Project, subject to meeting the Deliverables and Deadline specified in this Agreement.

## 10. Limitation of Liability

10.1. **Exclusion of Damages.** NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES ARISING OUT OF OR RELATING TO THIS AGREEMENT, REGARDLESS OF THE CAUSE OF ACTION OR THEORY OF LIABILITY.

10.2. **Cap on Liability.** EACH PARTY'S TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY UNDER THIS AGREEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL FEE SPECIFIED IN SECTION 3.1.

## 11. General Provisions

11.1. **Governing Law.** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of {{Governing Law State}}, without regard to its conflict of laws provisions.

11.2. **Dispute Resolution.** Any dispute arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall be resolved exclusively in the state or federal courts located in the State of {{Governing Law State}}, and each Party irrevocably consents to the jurisdiction of such courts.

11.3. **Notices.** All notices under this Agreement shall be in writing and shall be deemed given when: (a) delivered personally; (b) sent by confirmed email; or (c) sent by nationally recognized overnight courier.

11.4. **Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other Party.

11.5. **Severability.** If any provision of this Agreement is held to be invalid, illegal, or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.

11.6. **Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings.

11.7. **Amendment.** No amendment or modification of this Agreement shall be effective unless made in writing and signed by both Parties.

11.8. **Waiver.** The failure of either Party to enforce any provision of this Agreement shall not constitute a waiver of such Party's right to enforce that provision or any other provision in the future.

11.9. **Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original.
